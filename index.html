Middle Blue River Project Area 13

Project Manager: Khoa Nguyen

This project is being completed as required by the Federal Consent Decree. The project consists of design professional services for the review of CCTV inspections data (120,000 linear feet), manhole inspections data (540 manholes), and flow metering data, and the completion of smoke testing and dyed water testing. The design team will utilize the field investigation data to identify and quantify structural deficiencies in the sewer collection system for the development of cost effective repair and rehabilitation recommendations for reducing infiltration and inflow (I/I) in the Middle Blue River Basin. The design professionals will develop construction contract documents for the City's use in soliciting bids and implementing construction for the recommended sewer and manhole repair, rehabilitation, or replacement.
